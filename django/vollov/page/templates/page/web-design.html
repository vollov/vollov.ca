About Us

Our Customer

  • Companies who want increase the web traffic of existing web site.

Who may take full advantage of our services?

  • The businesses that need update their web sites often but suffered from high cost of maintenance.
  • The businesses starter who want build up a web site but don't know how to start.
  • The businesses web site who want to know where the customers comes from.
  • The businesses who want minimize the IT cost.

Our services

  • Customized Web Site design / software development.

How it works?

  1. Contact us by email or phone, and we meet to discuss your needs.

  3. We will show a prototype and customer decide if any thing need add to the web site.
  4. We finalize the requirements based on the prototype, and customer pay a portion to continue the project.
  5. Project go on line and close the deal.
